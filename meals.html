Weight Loss

Focus on high-protein, high-fiber meals that keep you full while maintaining a calorie deficit.

  • Grilled chicken salad with olive oil vinaigrette
  • Egg scramble with spinach and mushrooms
  • Roasted salmon with asparagus and quinoa

Weight Gain

Eat nutrient-dense, higher-calorie meals that combine proteins, healthy fats, and carbs.

  • Peanut butter banana smoothie with oats and Greek yogurt
  • Ground beef bowl with rice, avocado, and black beans
  • Whole wheat pasta with turkey meat sauce and cheese

Clean Eating

Meals focused on unprocessed, whole ingredients that nourish your body.

  • Veggie stir-fry with tofu and brown rice
  • Sweet potato, kale, and chickpea bowl
  • Oven-baked cod with lemon, herbs, and steamed broccoli
