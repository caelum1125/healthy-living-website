Daily Movement

Staying active improves circulation, boosts mood, and helps maintain a healthy weight. You don't need a gym — walking, stretching, or bodyweight exercises at home are great options.

Recovery

Sleep and rest are critical to your physical and mental health. Aim for 7–9 hours of quality sleep per night. Avoid screens before bed and try relaxing routines like reading or breathing exercises.

Discipline

Building small, consistent habits builds discipline — which keeps your goals on track. Examples: setting a sleep schedule, preparing meals ahead of time, and doing a short walk after meals.
